# How to Play

## Getting Started with 8-Bit Arcade

Playing 8-Bit Arcade is simple and free! Follow these steps to start earning crypto today.

### Step 1: Visit the Platform

Go to **[play.8bitarcade.games](https://play.8bitarcade.games)** in your web browser.

No download required - all games run directly in your browser!

> **‚ö†Ô∏è Desktop Required:** Our games are optimized for desktop/laptop play with keyboard controls. Mobile devices are not currently supported.

### Step 2: Connect Your Wallet

1. Click the **"Connect Wallet"** button in the top right
2. Select your wallet provider:
   - **MetaMask** (recommended)
   - **WalletConnect** (for mobile wallets)
   - **Coinbase Wallet**
   - Other Web3 wallets

3. Approve the connection request
4. Switch to **Arbitrum One** network when prompted

> **Don't have a wallet?** [Install MetaMask](https://metamask.io) - it's free and takes 2 minutes!

### Step 3: Set Your Username (Optional)

1. Click on your connected wallet address
2. Choose **"Set Username"**
3. Enter your desired username
4. Your username will appear on leaderboards instead of your wallet address

### Step 4: Choose a Game

Browse the game library and click on any game to play:

- **Space Rocks** - Asteroid shooter (medium difficulty)
- **Chomper** - Pac-Man style maze game (easy)
- **Block Drop** - Tetris-style puzzle (medium)
- **Brick Breaker** - Classic breakout (easy)
- **Pixel Snake** - Snake game (easy)
- **And 7+ more!**

### Step 5: Start Playing!

Each game shows:
- **üèÜ Top 3 Players** - Current leaderboard leaders
- **‚≠ê Difficulty Level** - Easy, Medium, or Hard
- **üìä Your Best Score** - Personal record

Click **"Play"** to start the game. Use keyboard controls to play.

### Step 6: Submit Your Score

When your game ends:
1. Your score is automatically submitted to the blockchain
2. Wait for transaction confirmation (~2 seconds on Arbitrum)
3. Check the leaderboard to see your ranking
4. Play again to improve your score!

### Step 7: Earn Daily Rewards

**Top 10 players** in each game earn daily rewards:

| Rank | Daily Reward | Monthly Potential |

| 1st | 12,500 8BIT | 375,000 8BIT |
| 2nd-5th | 6,250 8BIT | 187,500 8BIT |

Rewards are distributed **automatically every 24 hours** to your wallet.
