### Step 1: Prepare Your Wallet

1. **Install MetaMask** or another Web3 wallet
2. **Add Arbitrum One network** to your wallet
3. **Get USDC or ETH** on Arbitrum
   - Bridge from Ethereum using [Arbitrum Bridge](https://bridge.arbitrum.io)
   - Or buy directly on centralized exchange and withdraw to Arbitrum

### Step 2: Get USDC/ETH on Arbitrum

**Option A: Bridge from Ethereum**
- Use official Arbitrum Bridge
- ~10 minute bridge time
- Small gas fee on Ethereum

**Option B: CEX Withdrawal**
- Withdraw USDC from Binance, Coinbase, etc.
- Select "Arbitrum One" network
- Arrives in minutes
- Low withdrawal fees

### Step 3: Connect to Sale Site

1. Visit **[sale.8bitarcade.games](https://sale.8bitarcade.games)**
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"**
3. Approve MetaMask connection
4. Ensure you're on **Arbitrum One** network

### Step 4: Purchase Tokens

1. Enter amount in USD you want to invest (min $100)
2. Review token amount you'll receive
3. Click **"Buy 8BIT"**
4. Approve USDC/ETH spend in MetaMask
5. Confirm purchase transaction
6. Tokens sent to your wallet instantly!

### Step 5: Add Token to Wallet

