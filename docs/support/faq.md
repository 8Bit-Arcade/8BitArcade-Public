# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Quick Answers to Common Questions

### Getting Started

**Q: Do I need to invest money to earn crypto?**
A: No! Daily rewards are completely FREE. Just play games and rank top 10. Tournaments require entry fees but are optional.

**Q: What wallet do I need?**
A: MetaMask is recommended. Any Web3 wallet supporting Arbitrum One works. [Setup guide](../getting-started/connect-wallet.md)

**Q: Why Arbitrum instead of Ethereum?**
A: Much cheaper gas fees ($0.10 vs $5-50) and faster transactions (5 seconds vs minutes).

**Q: Do I need to KYC/verify identity?**
A: No. Wallet connection only. Anonymous and privacy-preserving.

**Q: Can I play from my phone?**
A: Yes! Mobile browsers work. Desktop keyboard recommended for competitive play.

### Earning Rewards

**Q: How much can I realistically earn?**
A: At $1M market cap: Top daily players earn $600-1,200/month. At $10M: $6K-12K/month. [See projections](../tokenomics/market-scenarios.md)

**Q: Are daily rewards really free?**
A: Yes! Just connect wallet and play. Small gas fee (~$0.10) to submit scores.

**Q: How often are rewards paid?**
A: Daily at 00:00 UTC. Automatically sent to your wallet within 5 minutes.

**Q: Can I rank in multiple games?**
A: Yes! Each game has separate leaderboards. Rank in all games = earn from all.

**Q: What if I'm not good at games?**
A: Practice mode is free. Even ranking 6th-10th earns rewards. Pick easier games.

**Q: Are tournaments worth it?**

**Q: What's the difference between Standard and High Roller?**

### Tokenomics

**Q: What's the total supply?**
A: 500,000,000 8BIT max supply. 200M initial circulation. [Full breakdown](../tokenomics/overview.md)

**Q: How does the buyback and burn work?**
A: 50% of tournament fees automatically swap to 8BIT and burn. Reduces supply permanently. [Details](../tokenomics/deflationary.md)

**Q: When do emissions end?**
A: Reward pool distributes over 5 years. After that, only staking emissions continue.

**Q: Is this inflationary or deflationary?**
A: Both. Years 1-5: Mild inflation from emissions. Year 6+: Pure deflation (burns only). If tournament volume high, deflationary even during emissions.

**Q: Is my wallet safe?**
A: Platform never holds your funds. You control your wallet. Use hardware wallet for large amounts.

**Q: What if I lose my seed phrase?**
A: Funds are lost permanently. No recovery possible. Write it down and store safely!

**Q: Can my account be hacked?**
A: Not unless someone gets your seed phrase. Never share it. Enable wallet password.

**Q: Is the platform a scam?**
