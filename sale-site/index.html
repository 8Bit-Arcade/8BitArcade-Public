FAQ

How do I earn 8BIT tokens?

Play any of our 12+ games daily and rank in the top 10 to earn free tokens. Or enter tournaments with bigger prize pools. No purchase necessary to start earning!

What blockchain is 8BIT on?

8BIT is built on Arbitrum One, a Layer 2 solution for Ethereum. This means lightning-fast transactions with minimal fees - perfect for gaming.

Are the games free to play?

Yes! All games are completely free to play with daily rewards. Tournaments have optional entry fees but are not required to enjoy the platform.

How does the token burn work?

50% of all tournament entry fees are permanently burned, reducing the total supply over time. This creates deflationary pressure and potential value appreciation.

What wallets are supported?

We support all major Web3 wallets including MetaMask, Rainbow, WalletConnect, and Coinbase Wallet through RainbowKit integration.

Is there a testnet to try?

Yes! We have a fully functional testnet on Arbitrum Sepolia with a faucet for free test tokens. Try everything risk-free before mainnet launch.

Can I play on mobile?

Our games are optimized for desktop/laptop play with keyboard controls. Mobile support is not currently available. For the best experience, please use a computer.
